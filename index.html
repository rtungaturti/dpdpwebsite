DPDP Compliance Maturity Levels

Our assessment categorizes your organization into one of five stages based on the DPDP Act 2023 framework:

Level 1: Reactive - Minimal documentation; privacy practices are unpredictable or missing.
Level 2: Defined - Basic documentation exists; processes are reactive and inconsistent.
Level 3: Strategic - Standardized, repeatable processes with defined roles like a DPO.
Level 4: Managed - Automated compliance monitoring; consent management is integrated.
Level 5: Optimized - Privacy is a core business driver; continuous risk-informed improvement.
